Compliance Statement

Our workflows align with the Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999, RBI Master Directions on Reporting under FEMA, Companies Act, 2013, and Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002. Every filing is validated against sectoral caps, pricing guidelines, and reporting timelines issued by RBI and DPIIT.

  • Four-eye review before submission on the FIRMS portal.
  • Secure coordination with AD Category-I banks, SEBI-registered custodians, and legal counsel.
  • Audit-ready documentation maintained for at least eight years per FEMA and Companies Act norms.

We continuously track circulars from RBI, MCA, and CBDT to keep our compliance library current so your filings remain defensible during inspections.
