Our Services

RBI FIRMS Portal Filings

Comprehensive reporting solutions for foreign investment transactions

Form FC-GPR

Required when a company receives foreign direct investment (FDI) under the general permission route.

Must be filed within 30 days of investment receipt

Form FC-TRS

Required for transfer of shares or convertible debentures between resident and non-resident entities.

Must be filed within 60 days of transaction

Form LLP-I

For reporting foreign direct investment in Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs).

Specific timelines apply

Form LLP-II

For reporting transfer of capital contribution between residents and non-residents in LLPs.

Specific timelines apply

Form DI

Declaration of Investment for downstream investment by an Indian company receiving foreign investment.

Must be filed within 30 days

Assistance with other RBI reporting requirements including amendments, revisions, and late filings.
